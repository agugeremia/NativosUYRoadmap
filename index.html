🌱 Nativos America Project - Uruguay

Interactive Roadmap for the Reforestation Program Implementation

🏗️
Phase 1: Kick-off and Setup
Establishing the legal and administrative structure, and selecting the technical team.
  • Legal and administrative setup
  • Team selection and training
  • Purchase nursery materials
🏭
Phase 2: Nursery Installation
Physical construction of the nursery and complete technological setup.
  • Nursery construction (germination, cultivation, warehouse)
  • Technological setup (Starlink, tablets, phones)
  • CertifyLab configuration and testing
👥
Phase 3: Community Training
Comprehensive community training in technology and native species management.
  • Workshops on App usage and nursery management
  • Specific training for women leaders
  • Creation of digital educational materials
🌳
Phase 4: Planting and Georeferencing
Start of mass planting with complete digital registration.
  • Initial planting of native trees
  • Photo-georeferencing with mobile app
  • Generation of digital certificates (NFT)
📈
Phase 5: Monitoring and Validation
Continuous monitoring and external validation of results.
  • Real-time tracking with CertifyLab
  • Annual external validations
  • Transparent publication of results
